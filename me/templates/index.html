About

MY WORK

I had the luck to be taught by/ and to work with very knowledgeable and intelligent people. I was always seeking for the best in the industry to learn from. That persuasion got me deeply involved into the German Python Community and brought me to San Francisco. Ever since I have started to program I have been working with Python and Django.

ME

I am a curious knowledge seeking and entrepreneurial woman – adventurous, live and tech lovin', active and ambitious. When I am not behind my Laptop working or checking out the latest technologies I like to fly my drone or to meet with real people (I actually really enjoy that ;-) ). I love what I am doing and am grateful for having such a fulfilled live. One of my personal projects is to help females getting involved in tech. It seems that especially females tend to be very sceptical about their tech skills and abilities (so was I for a long time). This is why I have founded the PyLadies Berlin. It is a very successful program and I love to see the women getting more confident and conscious about their skills. Also it got me more involved into the tech community, PyLadies Berlin is very proud that we were partner of the EuroPython 2014 and will be a partner of the PolyConf 2015 in Poznan.

Back-End Development

I love Python because of its well structured and readable code. It is a language used in various segments (e.g. Games, Science and Education), it is a powerful, fast and efficient programming language. Companies and Organizations like Soundcloud, Red Hat, Yahoo, Google and IBM are using Python. Django is a Web Application Framework written in Python. It follows the model-view-controller (MVC) pattern and makes you build clean, well structured Web Applications. Web Applications built in Django are scalable, can be of high complexity and are even for non techies easy to maintain and to work with.
